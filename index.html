One More Thing

Detective Kai Makana is drawn into a high-profile murder case in Honolulu, where all signs point to the victim’s lover—Eden Voss, a dangerously alluring woman with secrets to burn. As Kai digs deeper, he’s pulled into a seductive game of truth and lies, where every clue leads to more questions. Is Eden a cold-blooded killer or just a master of misdirection? In a world of desire and deception, one more thing could change everything.


TRAILER

CHARACTERS

A former surfer turned homicide detective, Kai Makana knows Honolulu’s dark side better than most. Known for his instincts and unorthodox methods, he’s sharp, relentless, and not easily shaken—until he meets Eden Voss. Drawn into a deadly game of lies and attraction, Kai must decide if he’s closing in on a killer—or falling under her spell.


Beautiful, mysterious, and dangerously intelligent, Eden Voss is the prime suspect in a high-profile murder. With a shifting past and a hypnotic presence, she manipulates every room she enters. Is she a misunderstood woman caught in the chaos—or a master manipulator with blood on her hands?


Keanu Kahele

Hawaiian-born Keanu Kahele is known for his rugged charm and commanding screen presence. A former surfer, he transitioned into acting with roles in indie crime dramas before breaking out as Detective Kai Makana in One More Thing. Charismatic and authentic, Kahele brings a natural intensity to every role he takes on.


Seraphina Vale

Seraphina Vale is a rising star celebrated for her layered, enigmatic performances. With a background in theater and a breakout role in Fatal Obsession, she shines as the seductive and dangerous Eden Voss in One More Thing. Elusive off-screen and magnetic on it, she’s Hollywood’s new noir icon.
